A Home Where the Ocean Meets Your Doorstep

Abades Ocean Deck House is a beautifully designed holiday home nestled in the tranquil coastal village of Abades, in the municipality of Arico, South Tenerife. With its spacious ocean-view deck, modern amenities, and proximity to pristine beaches, it's the perfect base for exploring one of the Canary Islands' best-kept secrets.

Things To Do in Abades & South Tenerife

From world-class snorkeling in marine protected areas to exploring mysterious abandoned ruins, Abades offers unforgettable experiences

#1 Activity

Snorkeling & Diving

Explore the crystal-clear waters of Abades marine protected area. See turtles, rays, and colorful fish just steps from the house.

🚶 2 min walk from house
Historic

Sanatorio de Abona (Leper Colony Ruins)

Explore the hauntingly beautiful abandoned leper sanatorium from the 1940s. A unique piece of Tenerife's history with stunning ocean views.

🚶 10 min walk
Beach

Playa de Abades

A tranquil, family-friendly beach with calm, clear waters. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and watching spectacular sunrises.

🚶 2 min walk from house
Excursion

Whale & Dolphin Watching

Spot pilot whales, bottlenose dolphins, and more on luxury yacht tours departing from nearby Costa Adeje and Los Cristianos.

🚶 25 min drive
UNESCO

Mount Teide National Park

Visit Spain's highest peak and UNESCO World Heritage Site. Cable car rides, stargazing, and volcanic landscapes await.

🚶 45 min drive
Adventure

Rock Climbing in Arico

Arico is Tenerife's premier climbing destination with 260+ routes through spectacular volcanic landscapes and ravines.

🚶 15 min drive
